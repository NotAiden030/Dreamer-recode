What is Dreamers Audio


Dreamers Audio Player is a project about Audioplayer creation.


This project isn't about video games or any other system, it's all about audio player creation.


We create and combine artwork with audioplayers, hope you will see our achievement in future.

Style | Visual

We've seen lots of speakers that are rectangle or cylindrical, but I thought about a new style that no one has ever made before! So I designed it hexagon! We're built different!

Appearance material

We create our speakers with laser-cutting technology. The materials are thin cut wood!

About name

The name of the Audio Player is called "Dreamers," no matter where you are, this audio player make you drop into the world of music.

About Speaker

With 1 bass boost speaker and 2 midrange speakers, We have 130w output, and dc 8V 5A for our input.

SIGNAL

We support mutil signal, almost every music signal type you could think of, also Bluetooth with Hifi, and more music custom systems.

Trusted

Don't worry about our Audio Player having safety concerns, it's fully safe with Power System Protection.

Customization

We support Bluetooth, you can also customize the base and main voice value on the AudioPlayer, creating your own style of listening experience.
